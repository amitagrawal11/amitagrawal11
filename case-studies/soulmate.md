# ❤️ Soulmate — Rethinking Modern Matchmaking

## The Story

While using popular matrimony and dating platforms, I noticed the same frustrating experience everywhere.

Users kept seeing the same inactive profiles for weeks. There was no way to know whether someone was genuinely active or had abandoned the platform months ago. People would wait for replies that were never going to arrive because the platform kept recycling stale profiles.

I believed technology could solve this.

Instead of building another dating app, I designed **Soulmate**—a cross-platform social and matrimony platform focused on helping users connect with active, compatible people nearby.

## What I Built

- 🟢 **Smart Profile Freshness** — Highlighted new, recently active, stale, and online/offline profiles.
- 📍 **Nearby Discovery** — Google Maps integration to discover profiles within configurable radiuses (10–50 km).
- 🤝 **Smart Match Score** — Compared two profiles, calculated compatibility, and explained why users matched (or didn't).
- ☕ **Event & Café Planner** — Allowed matched users to schedule meetings through an invitation and approval workflow.
- 💬 **Real-time Chat** — Secure one-to-one messaging powered by Firebase.
- 👆 **Swipe Discovery** — Modern swipe-based profile exploration for faster browsing.

## Outcome

The application remains a **private beta**. Running a real-time social platform requires continuous cloud infrastructure, messaging, and storage costs, so I chose to keep it private instead of releasing an under-supported product.

Today, a small group of friends actively use it within Firebase's free-tier limits, providing feedback that continues to shape the product.
